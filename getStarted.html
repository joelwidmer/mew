    Get Started

    It's easy! Follow these 3 steps.

    1

    Register your school or tax-exempt organization or check to see if your organization is already registered.

    2

    Save original UPC barcodes from eligible IGA Exclusive Brand products for proof of purchase. Your organization will earn $50.00 cash for each bundle of 500 original UPC labels sent to us with a label redemption form.

    3

    Make a difference! Your organization is free to spend toward organizational needs!
